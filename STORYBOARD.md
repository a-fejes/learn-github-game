## ğŸŒ€ Parallax & Visual Journey Design

### How Parallax Guides the Journey

**Level 0-1: Horizontal Scroll (Parallax Layers)**
- Foreground: Messy desk items (fast scroll)
- Midground: The repository structure (medium)
- Background: The digital landscape (slow)
- **Effect**: Shows depth of organizational layers

**Level 2: Vertical Parallax (Creating Content)**
- As user types, background scrolls
- Shows the "weight" of their creation
- Words lift up, background falls away
- **Effect**: Their voice becomes important

**Level 3-4: Deep Perspective Parallax**
- Forest scene with multiple depth layers
- Fork shows divergence (2D to 3D representation)
- Each layer represents a different aspect:
  - **Sky** (maintainers' decisions)
  - **Mountains** (the project scope)
  - **Trees** (contributors)
  - **Forest floor** (your local clone)
- **Effect**: Shows their place in the larger ecosystem

**Level 5: Isometric Parallax**
- Stage rotates as celebration happens
- Gives sense of "elevation" to the achievement
- Multiple characters at different depths celebrating
- **Effect**: You're at the center of a community
