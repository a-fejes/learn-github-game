A License tells people how they can use your work. MIT is free and simple. If you want a strong copyleft, start with GPL (or AGPL for networked apps). Creative Commons is great for writing and art.

Need a hint?

Edit any file in your fork (README.md is easiest), scroll to the commit form at the bottom, write a descriptive message (3-7 words), and click "Commit changes". We'll check if you have at least 2 commits in your fork!
